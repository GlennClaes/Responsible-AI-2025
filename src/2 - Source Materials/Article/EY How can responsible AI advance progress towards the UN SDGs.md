# How can responsible AI advance progress towards the UN SDGs

**Author(s):** Gillian Hinde
**Publication:** EY Global
**Date Published:** Aug 19, 2024
**URL:**  https://www.ey.com/en_gl/about-us/corporate-responsibility/how-can-responsible-ai-advance-progress-towards-the-un-sustainable-development-goals

## Summary

EY emphasizes the transformative potential of responsible artificial intelligence (AI) in accelerating progress toward the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The organization advocates for AI's role in addressing global challenges, enhancing inclusivity, and promoting environmental sustainability.

## Key Points

- **AI's Role in Advancing SDGs:** Responsible AI can expedite efforts to solve humanity’s most urgent problems, building a more sustainable and inclusive future.

- **Environmental Impact:** AI can unlock solutions to significant environmental challenges, contributing to climate action and sustainable resource management.

- **Social Innovation:** AI supports social innovators in addressing inequities, enhancing access to education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

- **Inclusive Development:** Collaboration among companies and social impact enterprises is essential to leverage AI-driven innovation for SDG progress.

## Quotes
> "The responsible use of AI unlocks unprecedented opportunities for social impact and is key to achieving the EY Ripples ambition of positively impacting one billion lives by 2030."
